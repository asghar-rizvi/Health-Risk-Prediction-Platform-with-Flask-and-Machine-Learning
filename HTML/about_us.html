about us

Welcome to Doctor Predictor, where we harness the power of machine learning to deliver accurate health predictions. Our platform uses advanced algorithms to assess risks for various health conditions, including heart attacks, diabetes, kidney disease, and liver disease. By employing models like Random Forest, Decision Tree, Logistic Regression, and K-Neighbors, we provide valuable insights to help you manage your health proactively. At Doctor Predictor, we are committed to empowering you with the knowledge you need to take control of your well-being.
